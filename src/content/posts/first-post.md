This is the first post on my personal blog. I'm building this as a minimal, typography-first space to share thoughts on design, development, and the things I'm learning.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Web development** — Astro, TypeScript, and modern tooling
- **Design** — Clean interfaces, readability, and intentional minimalism
- **Learning** — Notes and reflections from my journey

## Why a blog?

I believe in [learning in public](https://www.swyx.io/learn-in-public). Writing helps me think clearly and creates a record I can revisit. If it helps someone else along the way, that's a bonus.

Thanks for reading. I hope you find something useful here.
